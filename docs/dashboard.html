Cost and runaway protection

Past the soft ceiling the platform stops paying for the model and routes runs to a human. It never overspends, and it never silently drops an alert to save money.

Guardrails - what the safety net caught

The LLM is never trusted directly. Ungrounded citations are rejected and the deterministic analyser takes over - the human still gets alerted, and the override is recorded here rather than hidden.

Learning loop - driver precision from human verdicts

Drivers below the review threshold are automatically flagged for human verification on the next run. This table is measured, never hand-set.
